Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority at Travel4Fun.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Travel4Fun, we are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring a safe and secure experience while using our website. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data, as well as your rights regarding that data.

This policy applies to all visitors to our website, including those who submit forms, subscribe to our newsletter, or interact with our affiliate partners.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Form Submissions: When you submit our contact or newsletter forms, we collect your name, email address, and any additional information you provide (e.g., message content). These forms are processed securely via Netlify.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience and analyze site usage, as managed by our CookieConsent tool. You can accept or decline cookies via the popup on our site.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized data about your browsing behavior, such as pages visited and time spent on the site.
  • Affiliate Interactions: When you click affiliate links (e.g., Trip.com, Airalo), our partners may collect data as outlined in their privacy policies.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries submitted via our contact form.
  • Send you our weekly newsletter with travel tips and deals, if you’ve subscribed.
  • Improve our website through anonymized analytics.
  • Facilitate affiliate partnerships, earning commissions when you book through our links at no extra cost to you (see our Affiliate Disclosure).

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How We Protect Your Information

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • Secure Hosting: Our website is hosted on Netlify, which employs industry-standard security protocols.
  • Form Security: Form submissions are encrypted and protected against bots using Netlify’s honeypot feature.
  • Cookie Management: Our CookieConsent tool ensures cookies are only used with your consent.
  • Limited Access: Only authorized Travel4Fun team members can access form data, and only for the purposes outlined above.

While we strive to protect your data, no online system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and avoid sharing sensitive information unnecessarily.

Your Rights Under GDPR (EU Residents)

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (right to be forgotten).
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing under certain conditions.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for specific purposes, such as marketing.

To exercise these rights, contact us at travel4fun4u@tutamail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Your Rights Under CCPA (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Know: Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of your personal information (note: we do not sell your data).
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at travel4fun4u@tutamail.com. We will respond within 45 days.
